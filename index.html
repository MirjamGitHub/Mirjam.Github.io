Hi! I am Mirjam. I am a student Design for Interaction. Currently I am working on improving the experience for children on the Emergency Department of the Erasmus MC. Through emerging myself in the context I am trying to design a fitting interaction for both health caretakers and the visiting children. This project suits my main design goals: To reach social impact with my design, to design for happiness and to design with and for people. I am currently searching for a junior design function in social, sustainable or service design.
